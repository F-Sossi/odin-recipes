Churched Up Ramen

A simple way to make packaged ramen into a complete and delicious dish.

  • 2 Packages Ramen
  • 2 Baby Boc choi
  • 2 chicken thighs
  • Soy Sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. Season Chicken thighs with salt an pepper and saute in 1TBSP of Soy sauce for 8 minutes each side when done set aside to rest.
  2. While Chicken is resting steam boc choi and prepare packaged ramen according to direction on the package
  3. While the Ramen and boc choi cook slice chicken thighs thinly with the grain of the meat
  4. When Ramen is finished cooking add the sliced chicken and trimmed boc choy and enjoy your churched up ramen bowl
