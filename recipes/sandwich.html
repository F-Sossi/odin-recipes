Turkey Sandwich

A turkey Sandwich it is simple but good

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Bread of choice
  • BBQ sauce
  • Provolone cheese

Recipe

  1. Select 2 slices of bread and lay out next to each other
  2. Place 4 sliced of turkey on top of bread
  3. Top turkey with provolone cheese and spread BBQ on top of the cheese
  4. Finish sandwich with the other slice of bread slice in half and enjoy
